What We Do

Specialist Water Asset
Management Services

From diagnostic inspection through to high-pressure flushing and structural repair, we provide a complete pipeline maintenance solution for councils, contractors and infrastructure owners.

CCTV Pipe Inspection

High-definition camera surveys for stormwater and wastewater pipelines. We identify defects, blockages, root intrusions and structural failures with pinpoint accuracy.

  • Condition assessment for renewal planning
  • Push-rod & tractor camera systems
  • HD colour video with GPS tagging
  • Pipe inspection manual version 4
  • Diameter range: 75mm – 1800mm+
  • Post-inspection condition grading

Stormwater Inspection

Comprehensive condition assessments of stormwater drainage networks, culverts and catchpit laterals. We help asset owners understand network performance and plan renewals proactively.

  • Pre & post-flood assessments
  • Catchpit & manhole surveys
  • Sediment depth measurement
  • Asset register data capture

Wastewater Inspection

Detailed CCTV surveys of gravity sewers, rising mains and lateral connections to support maintenance planning, consent compliance and infrastructure renewals.

  • Lateral inspections
  • Pre-acceptance surveys
  • Infiltration & exfiltration detection
  • Full digital reporting suite

High-Pressure Flushing

Powerful hydro-jetting clears blockages, removes accumulated sediment and restores full pipe capacity quickly and efficiently — with minimal disruption to operations.

  • Combination jetter & vacuum units
  • Chain flail root cutting attachments
  • Grease & fat deposit removal
  • Pre-inspection flushing packages

Pipe Repair

Trenchless and excavation repair solutions to restore structural integrity and extend asset life. From spot repairs to pipe relining, we choose the right method for every situation.

  • Engineered repair solutions for drainage and pressure applications
  • Patch and point repairs for all pipe materials including PE and GRP
  • I&I full system sealing and repair
  • H2S resistant manhole and pump station coating

Condition Reporting

Clear, actionable reports with HD video, defect logs and prioritised maintenance recommendations. Our data helps you make confident decisions and meet regulatory requirements.

  • WINCAN reporting
  • GIS-integrated data export
  • Risk-ranked defect schedules
  • Custom reporting formats

Geospatial Services

Location intelligence that turns raw inspection data into actionable asset insights. We capture, process and deliver spatial data to help you understand, manage and plan your pipeline network with confidence.

  • GIS mapping & spatial data capture
  • GPS-tagged asset inventories
  • Interactive network mapping
  • GIS-integrated data export
  • Asset data quality assessments
  • Renewal planning spatial analysis

How We Work

Our Process

From initial enquiry to final report delivery, we follow a proven process that ensures quality, accuracy and clear communication at every stage.

1

Site Assessment & Scoping

We review your pipeline network, confirm access requirements, review safety requirements and develop an efficient inspection scope tailored to your objectives.

2

Pre-Work Inspection & Diagnostic

If necessary, we will undertake a detailed CCTV and physical inspection of your asset, diagnosing the issue and providing you with a full report of any potential remedial work needed.

3

Fix the problem - Repair, Clean or Flush

Where required, a pipe repair is conducted, debris is removed and cleared, or a high-pressure flush clears the line - to get water flowing again.

4

Post-Work inspection & Data Capture

Our trained operators conduct the post-work CCTV inspection to national coding standards, capturing high quality video, measurements and GPS coordinates in real time.

5

Reporting & Recommendations

You receive a comprehensive CCTV report with defect coding, photographic and video evidence, condition grades and clear maintenance recommendations — delivered promptly.
