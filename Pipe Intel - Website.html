What We Do

Specialist Pipeline
Services

From diagnostic inspection through to high-pressure flushing and structural repair, we provide a complete pipeline maintenance solution for councils, contractors and infrastructure owners.

CCTV Pipe Inspection

High-definition camera surveys for stormwater and wastewater pipelines. We identify defects, blockages, root intrusions and structural failures with pinpoint accuracy.

  • Push-rod & tractor camera systems
  • HD colour video with GPS tagging
  • WRC / NASSCO coded reporting
  • Diameter range: 75mm – 1800mm+
  • Post-inspection condition grading

Stormwater Inspection

Comprehensive condition assessments of stormwater drainage networks, culverts and catchpit laterals. We help asset owners understand network performance and plan renewals proactively.

  • Pre & post-flood assessments
  • Catchpit & manhole surveys
  • Sediment depth measurement
  • Asset register data capture

Wastewater Inspection

Detailed CCTV surveys of gravity sewers, rising mains and lateral connections to support maintenance planning, consent compliance and infrastructure renewals.

  • Lateral launch inspections
  • Pre-acceptance surveys
  • Infiltration & exfiltration detection
  • Full digital reporting suite

High-Pressure Flushing

Powerful hydro-jetting clears blockages, removes accumulated sediment and restores full pipe capacity quickly and efficiently — with minimal disruption to operations.

  • Combination jetter & vacuum units
  • Root cutting attachments
  • Grease & fat deposit removal
  • Pre-inspection flushing packages

Pipe Repair

Trenchless and excavation repair solutions to restore structural integrity and extend asset life. From spot repairs to full pipe relining, we choose the right method for every situation.

  • CIPP pipe relining
  • Patch repairs & point repairs
  • Joint sealing & grouting
  • Manhole rehabilitation

Condition Reporting

Clear, actionable reports with HD video, defect logs and prioritised maintenance recommendations. Our data helps you make confident decisions and meet regulatory requirements.

  • WINCAN / Pipe-IQ reporting
  • GIS-integrated data export
  • Risk-ranked defect schedules
  • Custom reporting formats

How We Work

Our Process

From initial enquiry to final report delivery, we follow a proven process that ensures quality, accuracy and clear communication at every stage.

1

Site Assessment & Scoping

We review your pipeline network, confirm access requirements and develop an efficient inspection scope tailored to your objectives.

2

Pre-Clean & Preparation

Where required, high-pressure flushing clears the line prior to inspection, ensuring the camera captures clear, accurate footage throughout.

3

CCTV Survey & Data Capture

Our trained operators conduct the inspection to national coding standards, capturing HD video, measurements and GPS coordinates in real time.

4

Reporting & Recommendations

You receive a comprehensive report with defect coding, video evidence, condition grades and clear maintenance recommendations — delivered promptly.
